FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions




Reservations / Bookings

  • How to make a reservation?

    Reservations can be made thru our booking channels: visit our website www.lagranderesidence.com then click BOOK Now, send message to our official Facebook account, e-mail us at reservations@lagranderesidence.com or contact us at +63 922-375-8679

    You can also find us on Airbnb – La Grande Residence account

  • Do you accept walk-in guests?

    We love to welcome everyone, but advance booking is advisable to ensure we have availability

  • How much is the security deposit per reservation?

    Per night it is Php 1,500 for either studio or 1-bedroom unit and Php 2,000 for 2-bedroom unit

  • How can I pay for my booking?

    We accept cash payment in the Reception but you can also pay thru bank transfer, wire transfer, cash deposit and credit or debit card.

    4% Surcharge applied for any card transaction

  • What are the inclusions for daily booking or per night stay? Does it include free breakfast and access to all facilities?

    Inclusive of room cleaning, water (we have filtered water from the tap), electricity, cable and internet and use of facilities

    Breakfast is not included in our daily rate, but we have Zermatt Restaurant at the Ground floor offering Breakfast to Dinner

  • Is pet allowed?

    We love pets, but for the comfort of all our guest, pets are not allowed.

  • Do you apply senior citizen discount?

    Senior Citizen discount can be applied on room accommodation but cannot be conjunction with another promo and/or 2 senior citizen discounts cannot be combined

  • How many pax is allowed per the unit?

    Maximum occupancy per unit: Studio and 1-bedroom unit – 2 pax and for 2-bedroom – 4pax

  • Can we add guest in the unit with additional charge?

    We do not charge additional fee for extra guest in the unit. We just limit to the maximum capacity to accommodate comfortably the maximum capacity informed above.

  • Are kids allowed?

    Kids are allowed when they are accompanied by their official guardian and parents. IDs must be provided.

  • Where are you located?

    Visit us at 46B Sarmiento Street, Plaridel 1, Malabanias, Angeles City, Pampanga

  • Can we bring food or cook inside the unit?

    Yes, you can bring your own food. You can even cook inside the unit. Each unit has its own kitchen where you have available refrigerator, microwave, induction cook, electric kettle, toaster and all utensils but as you are in staycation you can try our Zermatt Swiss Restaurant located at the Ground Floor

  • Does your unit have balcony?

    All units have balcony over viewing our ground floor pool

  • Do you have room with jacuzzi/bathtub?

    Book and relax in your own Jacuzzi of our Deluxe Studio unit and/or 1-bedroom suite and/or 2-bedroom units

  • Do you have units for sale?

    Please provide us your e-mail address so that we can endorse to concerned department for proper handling and accurate information. Or you may visit our website for updates

  • Do you provide Shuttle or Airport pick-up?

    We offer Shuttle Service...A comfortable & convenient shuttle. Booking in advance is recommended

  • Do you have ATM nearby?

    Yes, we do have it available in our Lobby

  • Is there parking available for guests?

    Yes, we do have it on a first come first serve

  • Is there generator in the building?

    Yes, there is. It brings back full power for the whole building including elevator, aircon in the unit, hot water for shower

Room Cleaning

  • How should I schedule for cleaning?

    Cleaning request should go through Reception for proper scheduling and handling.

  • Do I need to be in the unit during the cleaning?

    The vast majority of our residents/guests prefer not to be in the unit while the service is provided but if you want to be in the room is also a possibility. Just inform it at the reception while you check-in or any time before the service being provided.

  • What else do you offer besides regular cleaning?

    We do general/deep cleaning and sanitizing services, as well as disinfecting rooms with our UV lights prior any check-in.

  • What time does housekeeping start working?

    Our team starts their cleaning routine at 9AM

  • Can I reschedule or cancel my room cleaning?

    You can cancel your scheduled room cleaning any time but for rescheduling it should be done in advance to ensure LGR will have available staff to provide the service

  • Can I give my housekeeper cleaning instructions?

    Yes, as long it covers their scope of works. If you have cleaning preference that require extra time and procedures, minimal fee will be applied.

  • Can I book a room cleaning with the same cleaner?

    Yes, as long the staff is available on the day/time requested

  • Do you provide amenities in every room cleaning?

    Provision of room amenities like toiletries and soap vary on the Service Package you availed. For Service Package UPGRADE you may coordinate with Reception

  • Where can I give feedback about room cleaning?

    We love feedback as it help us to improve our service. You can scan the Room Cleaning QR code at the reception, or you can send us an email to reception@lagranderesidence.com

  • Is washing dishes included on the room cleaning service?

    Minimal fee applies for washing dishes. Avail the daily cleaning option thus, and this service will be included in the package

Laundry Service

  • Do you accept urgent/express laundry service, how much it will cost and how long it will take?

    Yes, we process express laundry and 50% additional from the basic charge will be applied

    A minimum of 4 hours is needed to process the express laundry

  • What is the Laundry operating hours?

    We are open from 9AM to 5PM.

  • Do you accept walk-in for laundry service or just for Resident only?

    We love to serve walk-in guests’ laundry as well

  • What are the other services you are offering aside from wash and folds?

    Other services we are offering wash & press, pressing only, dry cleaning, stain spotting and we can also wash your curtain and sofa cover

  • How long it will take for Regular Laundry?

    Laundry that will be picked-up before 10AM will be ready for delivery in your unit or pick-up at Reception the same day;5pm while, items collected/picked-up after 10PM will be available next day;12NN

  • How to request Laundry Service?

    Laundry pick-up or delivery can be coordinated with Reception. Just inform Reception if it will be picked-up in your unit

    For delivery, please instruct Reception if it will be delivered in your unit or to be left in Reception for your pick-up

  • How is your pick-up and delivery service?

    For pick-up; can be pick-up inside the unit as guests requested or in the reception area.

    For delivery; we can deliver directly to your unit as guests requested but if not, can be dropped in reception.

  • Do you accept hand wash and hang dry as special instruction for delicate item?

    Yes, but allow us to deliver your laundry after up to 2 days of pick-up
